ADVANCE ROBOTIC ARM

Our project showcases a precise robotic arm system, employing 2 MG995R servo motors and 3 mini servo motors for intricate movement control. Integrated ultrasonic sensors enable efficient object handling based on distance, managed by Arduino Uno for computations and PCA9685 16-channel servo driver for consistent power distribution.
